Crude Suicide rates vs GDP per Country (Year 2000)

By definition a crude suicide rate is the number of deaths in a year, divided by the population and multiplied by 100000. Generally we can see that Africa albeit having a lower average GDP per Capita their suicide rates are in the lower half. Europe with a higher GDP per Capita however has higher suicide rates.
